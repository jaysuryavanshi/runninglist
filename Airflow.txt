Open source platform that empowers data professionals to efficiently create, schedule & monitor the tasks and workflows.

Popular - Uses python, Robust integration, Highly scalable, Rich interface & Open source

D A G - Directed Acyclic Graph
Directed - Dependencies have specified direction
Acyclic	- No Cycle or Loops
Graph - Diagram that consists of Nodes (Tasks) & Edges (Dependencies)

Architecture

Users	-> WebServers -> Scheduler -> Executor -> Workers (Works with desired data platform to derive desired resuls)
